Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

To provide price tracking, we collect the following Discord data when a trade is logged:

  • Command usage timestamps and trade details (Item, Price, Qty)

Data is stored in a private database to calculate market averages, medians, and price trends. We do not sell or share this data with third parties.
